Repairs & Maintenance

Repair first, where it makes sense.

Not every problem needs a full replacement. If you have misted glass, stiff doors, failed hinges, damaged handles, draughts or a sliding door that no longer runs smoothly — a targeted repair is often quicker and cheaper than you think.

  • Misted or blown double-glazed glass units
  • Locks, hinges and handles — all makes supplied
  • Door alignment, adjustments and drop repairs
  • Sliding door running issues and track repairs
  • Draughts, seals and gasket replacements
  • General glazing faults and panel replacements
