About Us

A Different Kind of Learning — One That Works for Your Child

We believe every child deserves to enjoy learning. Here's how we make that happen.

Our Approach

Specialist, Therapeutic & Always Child-Led

At Wirral Rise, we believe every child deserves to enjoy learning. We provide specialist alternative provision for children aged 4–11 who are finding mainstream school overwhelming — whether that's due to anxiety, EBSA, demand avoidance or trauma. A diagnosis or label is not required — if a child is struggling, we want to help.

Our aim is always to help children return to their home school or move on to another suitable long-term setting when they feel ready — not before. We work at the child's pace, building confidence, trust and a genuine love of learning along the way.

Child-Led, Interest-Based Learning

Children help choose what they explore. That makes learning actually enjoyable — and it works.

Genuine Specialists Who Really Care

Our team includes neurodevelopmental practitioners, qualified teachers, a speech & language therapist and Cookie.

Always Working Towards a Happy Future

Everything we do supports children toward a safe, confident transition back when the time is right.
